## **1️⃣ General Pentesting & Red Teaming Documentation**
- **[MITRE ATT&CK Framework](https://attack.mitre.org/)** – Adversarial tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs).
- **[Red Team Field Manual (RTFM)](https://leanpub.com/rtfm)** – Concise reference for offensive security methodologies.
- **[Pentest Standard (PTES)](http://www.pentest-standard.org/)** – Structured penetration testing framework.
- **[NIST SP 800-115](https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/sp/800-115/final)** – NIST Guide to Information Security Testing & Assessment.
- **[Offensive Security’s PWK Labs Documentation](https://help.offensive-security.com/)** – Pentesting methodologies from OSCP.
- **[Red Team Alliance Methodology](https://www.redteamalliance.com/)** – Professional adversarial engagement methodologies.
